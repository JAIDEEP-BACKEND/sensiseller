Neo Entange X

Theoretical Advancements in Quantum Teleportation and IoT-Based Quantum-Linked Robotics

Author: JAIDEEP TAAK | CGC Landran

1. Introduction to Teleportation

Quantum teleportation uses entanglement for state transfer. While classical teleportation is science fiction, quantum mechanics opens possibilities for instant communication and matter state replication.

2. Teleportation Model

  • Quantum entangled chambers
  • Electromagnetic wave modulation
  • Tissue integrity analysis
  • Radio wave differentiation techniques

3. Quantum Entanglement

Entanglement allows particles to influence each other across distances. This principle is key for theoretical teleportation and secure communication.

4. IoT-Based Quantum Robotics

  • LoRa for low-power, long-range communication
  • ESP32-CAM for live streaming and AI
  • Bionic arms for remote manipulation
  • Quantum-linked glove and vehicle for control

5. Smart Glove Control

A glove embedded with quantum-linked particles and LoRa modules, enabling gesture-based control and global communication with haptic feedback.

6. Future Research

  • Machine learning for gesture recognition
  • Quantum encryption for secure data transfer
  • Multi-device entangled networks
  • Biological teleportation simulations

Conclusion

This research bridges quantum theory with IoT robotics. Though teleportation is still theoretical, the possibilities for future automation and secure quantum communication are immense.
